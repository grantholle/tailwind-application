Who I Am

I'm a full-stack developer with 9 years of experience. My hometown is Springfield, Missouri, but I've been living in Tianjin, China for close to 6 years. If selected for this job my family and I would return back to the US. What an opportunity!

Tailwind CSS and Refactoring UI (and now Tailwind UI) have radically changed how I build user interfaces. I would love to be on the team that brings so much power, flexibility and speed to creating and mainting interfaces for thousands of developers just like me.

Why I'm Applying

One of the big things I appreciate about Tailwind is the transparency of what you all are working on, the progress of your projects and your successes and failures along the way. I've learned a ton from Adam's screencasts and Steve's UI tips. My favorite newsletters to read were the ones leading up to the launch of Tailwind UI. Adam's details on the progress you all had made and some things you learned while building components were so insightful to me.

Things I'm looking forward accomplishing:

  • Being more community involvement. Being on the team would allow me to engage others in the community both from a support perspective and seeing the improvements that could be made from the suggestions of others.
  • Soaking up knowledge and wringing myself out. Having the opportunity to pair program with someone like Adam would be out of this world. I have so much more to learn from one of the greats and would love to share that knowledge with others.
  • Expanding Tailwind's horizons. Advancing adoption by adding integrations into more frameworks, tools and workflows.

I will never not use Tailwind. Going back to writing individual CSS declarations for every single aspect of my UI is laughable to me now. I believe that I have what it takes to join the team and play a part in continuing the success of Tailwind for years to come.
